Yukgaejang

Spicy beef and vegetable soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef brisket, cut into several pieces along the grain 3 inch long, soaked in cold water for 10 to 20 minutes, washed, and drained
  • 4 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 medium onion, cut in half
  • 12 ounces mung bean sprouts, washed and strained
  • 3-4 large green onions or 14-16 green onions, cut into 2.5 inch long pieces
  • 6 ounces of soaked(or fresh) gosari, cut into 2.5 long pieces
  • 8 cloves of garlic, minced

Sauce

  • 1/4 cup gochugaru
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (or cooking oil)

Directions

  1. In a large pot, bring 3 quarts (12 cups) of water to a boil. Add the beef along with the dried shiitake mushrooms and the onion.

    Cook for 1 hour over medium high heat

  2. Make the seasoning sauce

    Combine the sauce ingredients in a bowl and mix well

  3. Make the soup

    Remove the beef, onion, and mushrooms with a slotted strainer

    Let beef and mushrooms cool down, discard the cooked onion

    In a bowl, mix the vegetables with the sauce by hand until well incorporated, then add to the boiling stock

    Cover and cook 20 minutes over medium high heat until the vegetables are cooked through

    Slice the mushrooms and pull the beef apart into strips and add to the stock, cook another 10 minutes

  4. Finished!
